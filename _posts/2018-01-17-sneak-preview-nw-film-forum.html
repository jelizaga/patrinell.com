We would like to invite everyone to attend a special sneak preview screening of our film at the Northwest Film Forum on February 4! The Seattle Documentary Association will be showing a rough cut of our documentary for local filmmakers and a general audience through their "Works In Progress" program. The purpose of the screening is to obtain feedback and to gauge our audience's response. We will be presenting a shortened version of the film (<90 min) followed by a discussion period. Come meet and talk with the filmmakers—we would love to hear from you!

TIME: February 4, 4-6 PM
LOCATION: Northwest Film Forum
1515 12th Ave, Seattle, WA 98122
