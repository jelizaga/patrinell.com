Andrew Elizaga

Andrew's passion for filmmaking grew out of an interest in making short films about sea kayaking, wooden boatbuilding, and traditional arctic kayaks. He has directed a number of short films along with his partner Katya Palladina, including award-winning documentaries A Hospital in the Pines, Fake It Till You Make It, and Dancing With Hope. A devoted fan of guerilla-style documentary filmmaking, he draws inspiration from filmmakers Albert and David Maysles, Nick Broomfield, and Errol Morris.

Andrew currently resides in Tacoma, Washington, and is a practicing physician anesthesiologist.
