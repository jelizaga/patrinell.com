Interviews

The history of Rev. Patrinell “Pat” Staten Wright and the Total Experience Gospel Choir is filled with amazing stories—struggles, conflicts, failures, and triumphs—that occur against the background of the growth and decline of Seattle’s Central District as a uniquely African American community. Because it is impossible to depict all of these events in a feature-length film we are publishing here the full transcripts of the interviews conducted for the documentary as a supplement to the film. They provide an intriguing glimpse into the life of Pat Wright, her family, friends, and former and current members of the Total Experience Gospel Choir, through varied personal accounts and in rich detail.
